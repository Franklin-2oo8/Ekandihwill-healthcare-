About Us

Ekandiwill Physiotherapy Healthcare Services is a registered alternative medicine center (RC: 1062669) specializing in acupuncture and comprehensive natural health care.
Conveniently situated at along Ibom Specialist Road (near Eminence Hotel Sign Post) Uyo Nigeria, our center provides targeted acupuncture therapy for numerous health challenges, including:

  • Fibroids, ovarian, womb issues
  • Diabetes management and cholesterol control
  • Stroke rehabilitation and paralysis support
  • Chronic pain (waist, lumbar, stomach, toothache)
  • Respiratory conditions (asthma, cough)
  • Infections, malaria, and insomnia Kidney problems, stress, and menopause symptoms


Every treatment is tailored to the individual, with the goal of achieving meaningful and lasting improvement in your health and well-being. We warmly invite you to experience the transformative benefits of acupuncture at Ekandiwill Physiotherapy Healthcare Services.
